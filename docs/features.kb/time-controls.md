# Time Controls

Per-play timer to keep games moving.

## Configuration

- Default: 10 seconds per play
- Range: 5-30 seconds (host configurable)
- Passing phase: +50% time (default 15 seconds)

## Timeout Behavior

1. **First timeout**: System auto-plays a legal card for you
2. **Second timeout**: Forfeit (with brief grace period)

## Notes

Timer settings at host discretion. Competitive players prefer faster (5-10s).
